1)Traffic Light Control System (Completed)

The Traffic Light Control System was a hardware-based project designed to automate traffic signal operations. The system was developed to control the flow of traffic efficiently by following a predefined sequence of red, yellow, and green lights.

In this project, we designed and implemented the complete circuit, ensuring proper timing and synchronization of signals. The system helps in reducing manual intervention and improves traffic management at intersections.

2)Spring Quality Measurement System (Ongoing)

The Spring Quality Measurement System is an ongoing project focused on measuring the strength and quality of springs using electronic components and sensors. The objective of this system is to provide accurate and reliable measurement results.

This project involves analyzing the behavior of springs under different loads and converting physical measurements into readable data. It also focuses on improving precision and efficiency.
